Our Story

We are the Student Committee Board of Academy Federal School. We are a group of students who work together to make our school a fun and friendly place.

Our group started in 2024, June. Since then, we have been helping to make our school better every day.

We started the Student Committee Board because we wanted to help students share their ideas and make our school even better.

We plan fun events, help our classmates, and talk to teachers about what students need and want. Our job is to make school a great place for everyone.

You can find us at our school, Academy Federal School. Since our school is online school, we don't have an office where you can come and talk to us anytime.
